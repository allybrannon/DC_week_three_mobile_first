Strange that I stumbled upon this story. This is something that I think of often. As a vegetarian, I really try not to kill anything I come across. Except mosquitos and cockroaches. Ants, other flying bugs (not mosquitos), moths, bees... if I find them in my home, I try my best to take them outside where they are meant to be. Cockroaches and mosquitos- are not so lucky and usually die at first sight. I hope God (whoever) forgives me for those.
